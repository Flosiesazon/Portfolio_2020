Filmmaking

Music Video

D

uring the first semester of my 2nd year in Creative Digital Media, I had to film a music video. I decided to add this to my portfolio because it was challenging to complete as we were still on lockdown due to Covid pandemic. Furthermore, I did not own any professional camera therefore, I only used my phone to film. Finding a person to film with was not difficult although, setting up a schedule was hard because we were both students and we were only able to film at least twice a week due to Covid restrictions.

Overall, I am proud of what I have created as it improved my filming and editing skills at the end.
