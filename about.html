Flosie Sazon

The creator of FJ Portfolio

Hi everyone! I am the creator of the FJ portfolio website and currently studying in Technological University of Dublin, located in Blanchardstown campus. I am one of the students who did not start the course with a set of skills in every module and I had to start from scratch. Although it was difficult at first, I tried my best to learn everythig and prove myself that I can do it and always remember that even mistakes could lead into a better opportunity!

Inspiration

Mr Cronin

Apart from growing up surrounded by creative people in my family, there is a one more special person who inspired me to push myself on following my dreams and it was my art teacher, Mr. Cronin. His class has always been my comfort zone as secondary school was stressful and had a lot of pressure.

Mr. Cronin would stay for extra hours after school almost each day just to let his students work on their art projects and he would also buy some art supplies using his own money. As his student, I realised how much he loves teaching and has a great passion for art that he would sacrifice his own time for us. He was one of the first few people who believed in my abilities and that it is enough for me to find myself by choosing to study what I want without regret.
