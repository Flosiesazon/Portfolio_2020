BEST OF WORK

Brand Photography

O

f all the photographs I have taken since first year of college, this project was the one I am most proud of because this was the first time I did a brand photograph. It was the time when the lockdown started due to Covid pandemic therefore, I was not able to use professional cameras and my phone was the only option. At first, it was difficult as I must learn everything online and I also had to improvise the whole time such as using a lamp and phone flashlight to adjust the lighting or adding effects.

Before & After

T

o get the correct lighting for the images above, I had to take it from our kitchen as the lighting there was better. I also placed a silver paper to add the reflection effect on top of my A3 sketchbook that was wide open then placed the watch. By having the sketchbook wide open, I was able to use the other side of the page to act as my white background. After choosing the best photo, I opened it in Adobe Photoshop to retouch the photo and remove the scratches due to being used constantly. Since the photo was taken nicely, I didn't have to edit it much and I used a typography font that was similar to the original font of the product to add a more sophisticated and elegant feel to it.

I did not expect that I was able to produce something like this in the beginning because I don't even own a camera and I am still a beginner in photography. At the end, I am really proud of what I have done as all the challenges I experienced to get this far were all worth it.
